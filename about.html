Hi, I'm Andrew. I'm an Illustrator from Wellington.

Andrew James is a Wellington-based artist, illustrator, writer and educator. He usually has a pen in one hand and a sketchbook in the other.

He started drawing to record a long trip, but that turned into keeping a record of his whole life in sketchbooks and led to an obsession with all kinds of drawing.

Previously a communications meteorologist at MetService and a high school mathematics teacher, his science and education background lets him make complex ideas understandable with words and pictures.

Enjoying the serious as well as the silly, Andrew's career highlights working with Scion researchers on a comic about the smell of possum butts and travelling to Poland to teach at the Urban Sketchers global symposium.
