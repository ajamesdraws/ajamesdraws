Hi, I'm Andrew. I'm an Illustrator from Wellington.

I love drawing on location. I've been obsessively recording my life in little books for more than a decade.

I love making complex science understandable with words, drawings and humour. I'm a qualified meteorologist and I can talk about a lot more than just the weather./p>

When not drawing, you can find me chasing my kids, cooking elaborate meals, growing vegetables or making things out of wood in my little workshop.
