Hi, I'm Andrew. I'm an Illustrator from Wellington.

I love drawing on location. I've obsessively recorded my life in little books for over a decade.

I love making complex science understandable with words, drawings and humour. As a qualified meteorologist, I have science nous. And I can talk about much more than just the weather./p>

When not drawing, you can find me chasing my kids, cooking elaborate meals, growing vegetables or making things out of wood in my little workshop.
